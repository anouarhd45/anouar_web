COVID-19 Information

Stay Safe: Follow Guidelines and Maintain Social Distance

COVID-19 is an infectious disease caused by the SARS-CoV-2 virus. It spreads primarily through droplets of saliva or discharge from the nose when an infected person coughs or sneezes. The most common symptoms of COVID-19 include fever, dry cough, and tiredness. Some people may experience aches and pains, nasal congestion, sore throat, or diarrhea.

To protect yourself and others from the virus, it is important to:

  • Wear a mask in public places.
  • Maintain at least 6 feet of distance from others.
  • Wash your hands frequently with soap and water for at least 20 seconds.
  • Avoid touching your face, especially your eyes, nose, and mouth.
  • Get vaccinated if you are eligible.

For the latest information on COVID-19, visit the World Health Organization (WHO) website.
